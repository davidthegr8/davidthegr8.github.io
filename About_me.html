About me
I was born in Romania on May 27, 2002. When I was four and a half years old, I moved to the United States. I lived in Sunnyside and attended a few schools until third grade when I moved to Long Island City along with my parents, aunt and uncle. I went to P.S. 78, a school near my house for elementary school and then went to I.S. 141 for middle school, where I met lots of new people. In eighth grade, I took the SHSAT and got accepted into Stuyvesant. During the summer, I went to a program named Kinet-X where I learned Electrical Engineering and programming. If you want to see what I learned at Kinet-X, visit my Kinet-X page.
